❔ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deletion take?

Once you verify your email, your data is deleted immediately from our servers. Verification and processing typically takes 7 business days.

Can I cancel after requesting deletion?

Yes, you have 24 hours from when you receive the verification email to cancel your deletion request by clicking the cancel link in that email.

What data gets deleted?

All personal data associated with your account is permanently removed, including your profile, vehicle details, subscription history, cleaning records, and payment information. See our Account Deletion Policy for complete details.

What about payment records with Razorpay?

ZepWash deletes all records on our side. Razorpay (our payment processor) retains records per their data retention policy. You may contact Razorpay directly if needed.
